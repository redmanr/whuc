Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to share the good news of Jesus Christ, as part of the United Church of Canada in North Burnaby, through worship, pastoral care, outreach, and wise stewardship of people and resources. Our aim is to be a spiritually nurturing community of faith, seeking opportunities to develop a multicultural ministry. To fulfill this mission we offer:
  • Intergenerational worship
  • Children's Sunday morning program
  • Choir practice
  • After-church fellowship
  • Monthly birthday cakes
